About Us

Welcome to PowerHousing! Our goal is to provide consumers looking to move or purchase a home with agency in the earliest stages of the process.

What We Do

PowerHousing provides a simple calculator and map that allows you to provide essential income and expense information and compare it against the median price of certain housing types in the Tampa Bay area. All calculations for registered users are automatically saved so you can view them at any time. This lets you get a first look at your financial situation in comparison to the cost of housing, and gives you the power to do a bit of exploratory research without facing endless pages of listings or messages from a realtor.

What We Don't Do

PowerHousing is not a listing service. There are no apartments or houses to examine or set up visits for. We are not a realty service. We cannot advise you on mortgage rates or closing costs. We cannot calculate tax or insurance information for you. However, if you know that information, you can absolutely enter it in the expenses field.

Pricing Data

Median One Bedroom and Two Bedroom Apartment pricing data was taken from Zumper at https://www.zumper.com/rent-research/ in the Summer of 2024.

Median House pricing data was taken from Redfin at https://www.redfin.com. Redfin also has a more advance Affordability Calculator, as well as a Mortgage Calculator. These are available at https://www.redfin.com/how-much-house-can-i-afford and https://www.redfin.com/mortgage-calculator
